Scrum, Kanban methodology, SAFe, and LeSS all have their own certifications. A group of practicing managers organizes our certification path to compete in this challenging consultancy area.

Reading minimum:

Part. Basics

  • An Introduction to Cybernetics. Author: W. Ross Ashby
  • Fundamentals of Queueing Theory. Authors: Shortle, John F. - Thompson, James M. - Gross, Donald - Harris, Carl M.
  • The Essential Deming: Leadership Principles from the Father of Quality. Author: Deming, Edwards W.
  • Management: Tasks, Responsibilities, Practices. Author: Drucker, Peter F.
  • On Purposeful Systems: An Interdisciplinary Analysis of Individual and Social Behavior as a System of Purposeful Events. Authors: Russell L. Ackoff - Fred E. Emery

Part. People

  • Organizational Behavior by OpenStax. Authors: J. Stewart Black; David S. Bright; and others

Part. Strategy

  • Good Strategy Bad Strategy: The Difference and Why It Matters. Author: Richard Rumelt

Exams:

Examinations happen on a per-part basis once per month before practice with groups of the required size. They are remote video calls. Only written notes are allowed. Groups of candidates discuss the cases together.

Practice:

6-month supervision. Only proven results records are permitted.
